\plainlanguagesummary{
From January 2024, all FARs must include a ``plain language summary''.

Guidelines for the plain language summary are as follows:
\begin{description}
  \item[Placement:]{within the report after the Table of Contents and before Executive Summary with the latter starting on page 1}
  \item[Length:]{short and concise, can use bullet points, no more than a sentence each}
  \item[Target audience:]{newspaper reading level (13 y), no jargon, or define if necessary}
  \item[Content:]{brief intro. of subject matter, what did we do, why did we do it, what did we learn. Stick to key results of interest.}
\end{description}
}
